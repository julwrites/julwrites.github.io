## Workflow System

### 1. Seed Collection
- **When**: Anytime (working, commuting, thinking)
- **Format**: "Jiro, seed for [topic]: [your thought]"
- **Action**: I log in seeds.md and associate with relevant draft

### 2. Drafting Turn (Weekly)
- **When**: Sunday afternoons or preferred quiet time
- **Action**: "Flesh out the seeds" command
- **Result**: Raw thoughts → coherent drafts in your voice

### 3. Editorial Pass
- **Time**: 15-20 minutes per draft
- **Focus**: Add "heart" and nuance that AI might miss
- **Check**: Ensure "Personal Story → Technical Reflection" arc

### 4. Review & Publish
- **Review**: Sub-agent tone check
- **Publish**: Move from drafts/ to blog/ folder
- **Version**: Keep draft versions for reference

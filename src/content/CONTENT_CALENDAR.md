## Workflow System

### 1. Seed Collection
- **When**: Anytime (working, commuting, thinking)
- **Format**: "Seed for [topic]: [your thought]"
- **Action**: Logged in `seeds.md`, associated with relevant draft

### 2. Drafting Sessions (During Sync-ups)
- **When**: 9 AM & 9 PM daily (cron jobs set)
- **Action**: Flesh out seeds, work on scheduled drafts
- **Focus**: Current week's priority draft

### 3. Editorial Pass
- **Time**: 15-20 minutes per draft
- **When**: Before publication date
- **Focus**: Personal stories, nuance, "heart" of the piece

### 4. Review & Publish
- **Review**: Ensure "Personal Story → Technical Reflection" arc
- **Publish**: Move from `drafts/blog/` to `blog/` on publication date
- **Version**: Keep draft for reference
